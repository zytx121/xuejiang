Junfeng Wang received the B.S. degree from the Beijing University of Technology in 1993, the M.S. degree from the Institute of Electronics, Chinese Academy of Science in 1996, and the Ph.D. degree from the University of Massachusetts at North Dartmouth in 2002, all in electronic engineering. He was a research assistant at the Institute of Electronics, Chinese Academy of Science from 1996 to 1998, and a postdoctoral research fellow at the University of Michigan at Ann Arbor from 2002 to 2003. Since 2003, he is an assistant professor, an associate professor, and a professor successively at Shanghai Jiaotong University. His interests include radar imaging, medical imaging, and astronomical imaging, with emphasis on signal processing.
